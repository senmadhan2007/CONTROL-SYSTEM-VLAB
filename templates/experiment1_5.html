Experiment 1.5 – Step Response of Second Order System

Reference Program

s = poly(0,'s');
t = 0:0.05:30;
Sys_2 = syslin('c', 1/(s^2 + 7*s + 10));
Step_G_s = csim('step', t, Sys_2);
plot2d(t, Step_G_s);
xgrid();
title("Step Response of Second Order System");
xlabel("Time in Sec.");
ylabel("Response");
